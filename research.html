Research Interests

My research lies in the broad area of geometry and topology, particularly:

  • Fuchsian groups — discrete subgroups of PSL(2, ℝ) and the hyperbolic surfaces they uniformize; reciprocity and z-classes in these groups.
  • Reciprocal geodesics — growth and combinatorial structure of reciprocal classes in Hecke groups and the modular group.
  • Mapping class groups and Teichmüller space — deformation spaces of hyperbolic structures on surfaces.
  • Hyperbolic 3-space — discrete subgroups of PSL(2, ℂ), reversibility in fundamental groups of Seifert-fibered spaces and other 3-manifold groups.
