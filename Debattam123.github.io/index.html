About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, working with Dr. Bidyut Sanki. Before this, I was a Visiting Scientist in the Stat-Math Unit at the Indian Statistical Institute Kolkata.

I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at IISER Mohali (2020–2025) under the supervision of Prof. Krishnendu Gongopadhyay. My thesis was titled “Reciprocity of Discrete Subgroups of PSL(2, ℂ)”.

My research lies in geometry and topology — particularly Fuchsian groups, mapping class groups, Teichmüller space, and hyperbolic 3-space.
